Privacy Policy for Furtherance™

  • This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information of its users.
  • Entries are stored locally on your device. Your data is encrypted on device as per standard Apple practices. Your data is not sent anywhere over the network.
  • The developer does not have access to your data.
  • Any of your entries older than 4 weeks are automatically pruned by the app.
  • No analytics or tracking are used in this app. The graphical insights in the app are calculated locally on-device and on-demand.
  • Ads are served on your device using Google AdMob and are subject to Google's privacy policy. Ads can be removed if so desired via a one-time payment.
