Key Features

  • Add activities to 10 provided categories
  • Activities can be planned (toggle done or not done yet) or logged (after the fact)
  • Add and delete your own categories as you please
  • Assign a polarity to your categories as you please - every category can be set as 'dial up' or 'dial down', letting you focus on maximizing or minimizing activities in that category
  • Update polarities any time you want
  • Get motivated by about a 100 randomly appearing motivational quotes on a beautiful dynamic stained glass background
  • View the past 4 weeks of your productivity journey in the history tab in a visually stunning heat map as well as a listing of activities
  • Every Monday, review your past week and assign yourself grades in every category you care about. Optionally add notes to yourself to self-direct and course-correct your next week
  • Rest assured of high data privacy - data lives only locally on your device and not sent anywhere
  • Choose from 4 header fonts, 4 body fonts, 2 color schemes, and 6 accent colors to make your tracking experience unique to you
  • Use it on iPhone, iPad, or macOS
  • Free to use (with ads) - option to remove ads with a one time payment for lifetime access
