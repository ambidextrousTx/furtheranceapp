Key Features

  • Journal your way to increased contentment - whether you're 20 years old or 120 years young
  • Add entries to 22 powerful, insightful gratitude categories as you grow your collection of things to be grateful for. Add custom categories of your own
  • Rest assured of high data privacy - data lives only locally on your device and in iCloud (iCloud sync can be turned off any time). Delete your data any time you want
  • Navigate easily and intuitively. Quickly search within your data. Helpful tips are strewn throughout and available on demand
  • Export your data as CSV
  • Add, bookmark, edit, delete, or reorder all entries
  • Add, bookmark, edit, delete, or reorder custom categories, and bookmark or reorder system categories
  • View a heatmap of your journaling habit and a pie chart of your most used categories
  • Choose from 4 header fonts, 4 body fonts, and 2 color schemes for a personalized journaling experience
  • Use it on iPhone (voice input support), iPad (voice input and Pencil support), and macOS (select keyboard shortcuts)
  • Use accessibility features (reduced motion, dynamic text sizing, good voiceover coverage for the visually impaired) and custom reminders
  • Localized in 3 languages (English, Deutsch, Español) depending on your device’s language settings
  • Journal in any language you want regardless of localization support - a boon for language learners
  • Write freely and profusely - stress-tested with tens of thousands of entries and with entries up to 10,000 words
  • Write distraction free in a reflective, meditative state of mind
